My Education

SKK SK Paya Rumput , Melaka

I attended Sekolah Kebangsaan Paya Rumput (SKK SK Paya Rumput), located in the historic state of Melaka, from 2011 to 2016. During my time there, I was proud to serve as a Pengawas Pembimbing Rakan Sebaya (Peer Guidance Prefect), a role that strengthened my leadership and communication skills. Known for its focus on Science and Rugby, the school provided a dynamic environment that encouraged both academic and co-curricular excellence. These formative years not only nurtured my curiosity in learning but also instilled values of teamwork and resilience, shaping the foundation of my personal growth.

Sekolah Sains Sultan Haji Ahmad Shah Pekan , Pahang

I attended Sekolah Sains Sultan Haji Ahmad Shah Pekan (Shah Pekan), a prestigious Sekolah Berasrama Penuh (SBP), from 2017 to 2022. Renowned as one of the largest schools in Southeast Asia, Shah Pekan provided an exceptional environment for academic and personal development. In Form 4, I pursued the Social Science stream, which deepened my understanding of societal structures and human behavior. The school’s emphasis on discipline, leadership, and excellence challenged me to grow both as a student and an individual. My years at Shah Pekan were filled with valuable experiences that continue to inspire and shape my journey.

Universiti Teknologi Mara Cawangan Kelantan

I am currently pursuing my diploma in Information Management at Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) Machang, where I began my studies in 2022. Now in my final year, my time here has been a transformative journey of academic growth and self-discovery. UiTM Machang has provided me with a strong foundation in information science, research, and digital management while encouraging creativity and innovation. Beyond academics, the supportive environment and engaging campus life have enriched my experience, preparing me for future challenges and opportunities in my field.
